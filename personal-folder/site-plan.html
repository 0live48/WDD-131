Prashab Gharti Chetri

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as my personal online resume and portfolio. It will highlight my background, skills, education, work experience, and the projects I’ve created. The site will give employers, collaborators, and clients a clear overview of who I am and what I can do.

Audience

The intended audience includes future employers, internship recruiters, professors, collaborators in technology and media, and anyone interested in my work as a computer science student, videographer, and content creator.

Dynamic Elements (JavaScript)

JavaScript will be used to add interactivity across multiple sections. Examples include:

  • Dynamic “Skills” filter (filter skills by category using arrays & DOM updates)
  • A theme switcher (light/dark mode using conditional statements)
  • A “project viewer” that loads project info from an array of objects
  • Dynamic year footer and “Last updated” label
  • A contact form acknowledgment popup using event listeners

Content

Home Page

The home page will briefly introduce who I am with a clean hero section, my name, picture, and a short description of what I do. It will feature links to my resume, projects, and contact section.

About Page (Part of Home Page or Child Page)

This section will describe my background: growing up in Nepal, serving a mission in India, studying at BYU–Idaho, and developing skills in computer science, videography, and content creation.

Projects Page (Child Page)

This page will highlight:

  • The Elemental Nexus – my web-based project/game concept
  • Printiology – printing service and brand I helped build
  • Other school, web development, and media projects

Each project will load dynamically from a JavaScript array of objects.

Contact Section

Simple contact form with JS validation and a success message popup.
