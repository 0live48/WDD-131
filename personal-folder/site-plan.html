Prashab Gharti Chetri

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as my personal online resume and portfolio. It will highlight my background, skills, education, work experience, and the projects I’ve created. This site will give employers, collaborators, and clients a clear overview of who I am and what I can do.

Audience

The intended audience includes employers, internship recruiters, professors, collaborators in technology and media, and anyone interested in my work as a computer science student, videographer, and content creator.

Dynamic Elements (JavaScript)

JavaScript will be used to add interactivity across the website, including:

  • A dynamic skills filter (filter skills by category)
  • Theme switcher (light/dark mode)
  • Project viewer that loads project data from a JS array
  • Dynamic copyright year and “Last updated” text
  • Contact form success message using event listeners

Branding

Website Logo

I will use my own picture/logo as the branding image for the site.

Content

Home Page

The home page will introduce who I am with a clean hero section featuring my picture, name, and a short description of what I do. It will also include links to my resume, projects, and contact section.

About Page (Home Section or Child Page)

This section will describe my background: growing up in Nepal, serving a mission in India, studying at BYU–Idaho, and developing skills in computer science, videography, and content creation.

Projects Page (Child Page)

This page will include projects such as:

  • The Elemental Nexus – my web-based project/game concept
  • Printiology – a printing service and brand I contributed to
  • Other web development, media, and school projects

All project details will be dynamically loaded from a JavaScript array of objects.

Contact Section

A simple contact form with JavaScript validation and a confirmation message.
